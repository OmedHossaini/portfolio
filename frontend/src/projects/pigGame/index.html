Welcome to the Pig Game!

How to Play:

Rolling the Dice:

You start your turn by rolling the dice. To roll, click the "Roll Dice" button. The number rolled on the dice will be added to your current score.

Current Score:

Your current score is the sum of the dice rolls during your turn. Be strategic and decide when to "Hold" to add your current score to your total score.

Holding Your Score:

Click the "Hold" button to add your current score to your total score. It's crucial to hold before you roll a 1, as rolling a 1 will reset your current score to zero, and you lose your turn.

Winning the Game:

The game continues with players taking turns. The first player to reach or exceed 20 points wins the game.

Additional Notes:

Rolling a 1:

If you roll a 1, your current score resets to zero, and your turn ends. Be cautious and decide wisely when to roll the dice and when to hold.

Switching Turns:

Turns alternate between players. The active player is indicated by the highlighted section on the game screen.

Enjoy the Pig Game! May the dice be in your favor. Good luck!
